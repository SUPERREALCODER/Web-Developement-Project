Product Description

Introducing our latest addition to the mid-century inspired office furniture collection - the versatile and stylish chair. This chair is part of a beautiful family of furniture that includes filing cabinets, desks, bookcases, meeting tables, and more. Designed with both home and business settings in mind, this chair is suitable for any environment.

With several options of shell color and base finishes, you can customize this chair to perfectly match your existing decor. Choose between plastic back and front upholstery or full upholstery in a variety of fabric and leather options. The base finish options include stainless steel, matte black, gloss white, or chrome. You can also choose whether to have armrests or not, depending on your preference.

Constructed with durability and comfort in mind, this chair features a 5-wheel plastic coated aluminum base for stability and mobility. The pneumatic chair adjust allows for easy raise and lower action, ensuring optimal comfort for extended periods of sitting.

With its sleek design and high-quality materials, this chair is not only visually appealing but also built to last. The shell is made of cast aluminum with a modified nylon PA6/PA66 coating, providing strength and durability. The seat is made of HD36 foam, offering a comfortable seating experience.
